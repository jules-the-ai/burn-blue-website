Burn.Blue respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can contact us with questions.

## Information we collect

We do not intentionally track visitors across the web, sell personal information, or run advertising pixels on this website.

We may collect information you choose to send us, such as your name, email address, business name, project details, and any other information included in a contact form, email, or direct message.

Like most websites, our hosting and infrastructure providers may automatically process basic technical information needed to deliver and secure the site, such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, and server log data.

## How we use information

We use information only for ordinary business purposes, including to:

- respond to inquiries;
- discuss or deliver web design, development, AI assistant setup, automation, and related services;
- maintain, secure, and improve the website;
- comply with legal obligations; and
- prevent abuse, spam, or security issues.

## Cookies and analytics

Burn.Blue does not currently use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

If analytics are added in the future, they will be used to understand site performance and improve the website, not to sell personal information.

## Sharing information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with service providers that help operate the website, communicate with clients, or deliver requested services. These providers are expected to use information only as needed to provide their services. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or systems.

## Data retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law, accounting, dispute resolution, or legitimate business needs.

## Your choices

You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have provided to us. We may need to retain certain information when required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

## Third-party links

This website may link to third-party websites or services. Burn.Blue is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
