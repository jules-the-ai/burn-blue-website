## AI assistants that do useful work, not just chat
Burn.Blue helps businesses set up practical AI assistants that can search knowledge, draft messages, summarize documents, monitor inboxes, and support daily operations.

### What this includes
- AI assistant use-case discovery
- Account, permission, and tool setup
- Knowledge source and document connection
- Prompt and workflow design

### Example use cases

- Meeting notes and follow-up summaries
- Internal knowledge search
- Lead intake review
- Drafting proposals, SOPs, and customer messages
