## Automate the repetitive work around the website
Many businesses lose time after a lead form is submitted: copying data, sending follow-ups, scheduling calls, updating spreadsheets, and notifying the right person. Burn.Blue designs automations that close those gaps.

### What this includes
- Workflow mapping and automation planning
- Form-to-email and form-to-CRM flows
- Scheduling and reminder automations
- Document and proposal generation
- Notifications, reporting, and approval workflows

### Automation principles
Good automation should be observable, reversible, and easy to understand. Burn.Blue focuses on small reliable systems that save time without creating black boxes.
