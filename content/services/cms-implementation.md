## A content system your team can keep using
Burn.Blue uses Hugo to create fast sites with clean content structure. Pages live as manageable Markdown files, making the site easier to edit, review, version, and publish.

### What this includes
- Hugo project setup
- Content sections and front matter
- Reusable layouts for service pages
- Navigation and taxonomy planning
- Documentation for future updates

### Why Hugo
Hugo is fast, reliable, portable, and well suited for professional marketing sites that do not need heavy database infrastructure.
