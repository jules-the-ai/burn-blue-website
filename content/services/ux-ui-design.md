## Make the next step obvious
Good UX makes a site or app feel simple, even when the business behind it is complex. Burn.Blue maps decisions, removes friction, and designs interfaces that support real user behavior.

### What this includes
- User journey mapping
- Wireframes and clickable prototypes
- Interface design systems
- Form and booking flow optimization
- Usability improvements for existing pages

### Outcome
Visitors understand what you do, why it matters, and what to do next.
