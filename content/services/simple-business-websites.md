## A simple website without the big upfront project

This service is for small businesses that need a credible informational website quickly: a clean homepage, basic service information, contact details, and a professional place to send customers.

Instead of a large design project, Burn.Blue keeps the scope intentionally simple so the site can launch quickly and stay affordable.

### What is included

- A simple informational website for **$30/month**
- **48-hour turnaround** after Burn.Blue has the business details, copy, photos, and any required approvals
- Free standard domain name registration included
- Free website hosting included while the plan is active
- Mobile-friendly layout
- Basic search-friendly page title and description
- Contact link or simple contact section
- Basic launch support and lightweight maintenance for the hosted site

### Best fit

This is a good fit for small businesses, solo operators, local services, new projects, and simple brands that need a professional web presence but do not need custom software, ecommerce, complex booking, memberships, or a large content strategy.

### Domain name note

The included free domain covers a standard available domain name at normal registration pricing. Some names are considered premium domains, brokered domains, already-owned domains, or unusually expensive specialty names. If your first choice falls into one of those categories, Burn.Blue can help you choose a practical alternative or quote the extra domain cost before anything is purchased. Hosting is included while the monthly plan is active.

### What is not included in the simple plan

- Premium or unusually expensive domain names
- Ecommerce stores or online payments
- Custom web applications, portals, or dashboards
- Large multi-page content builds
- Advanced SEO campaigns or ongoing content writing
- Paid advertising management

Those can still be scoped separately if you need them.
